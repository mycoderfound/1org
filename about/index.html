About myCoder

Empowering Digital Futures. Together.

Welcome to myCoder — the central hub connecting every branch of the myCoder Foundation movement. We're on a mission to transform disconnected communities through digital skills, creative opportunities, and inclusive tech education. Whether you're a curious youth, an aspiring entrepreneur, or a tech-savvy creator, there's a place for you in the myCoder ecosystem.

myCoder Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to teaching digital literacy, coding, and online safety to underserved youth. With free Club Memberships and community workshops, we help young learners build confidence and digital skills that prepare them for the future.
